COUNTING COUP MEDIA

Counting Coup Media is a group of ambitious filmmakers who are focused on bringing a new kind of quality and style to the Native community. With over ten years of experience, they can handle any project that hits their table.

The company is currently working in association with the Kalispel Tribe, the Spokane Tribe, and Kamas Path.
